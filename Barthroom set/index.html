How it's made

The making of a
Stillwater tub.

Every piece passes through the same four hands before it reaches yours.

01 — Quarry

Sourced in slabs

Travertine and marl are cut in single slabs from quarries we've worked with for over a decade, chosen for grain and mineral character rather than uniformity.

02 — Carve

Shaped by hand and lathe

Each tub is roughed on a stone lathe, then finished entirely by hand — a process that takes a single carver nearly three weeks.

03 — Seal

Sealed, not painted

A breathable mineral sealant protects the stone while letting its natural texture and temperature come through against skin.

04 — Rest

Cured for fourteen days

Before it ever ships, every piece rests in our studio for two full weeks — our only real quality check.
